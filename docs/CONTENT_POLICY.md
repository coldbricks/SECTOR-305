# Content policy

## Allowed

- Fictional names, phones, addresses, plates  
- Training-safe violence **described** as CAD natures (robbery, weapons flag) without gore  
- Language friction (EN/ES/Creole) as **timer/accuracy systems**, not stereotypes  
- Stress via concurrency, incomplete info, radio pressure  

## Forbidden

- Real PII or thinly anonymized real incidents  
- Licensed IAED/APCO/NENA card text or exam items  
- “Official certification” claims or agency logos implying endorsement  
- Gore tourism, sexual violence as entertainment, suicide mini-games  
- Shock audio designed to traumatize rather than train protocol  
- Stereotyped caller comedy  

## Trauma-aware defaults

- Structured modules with content notes in scenario JSON  
- No autoplay extreme distress audio without user gesture (when audio ships)  
- Difficulty = cognitive/policy load, not graphic detail  

## Open-source misuse

README states training purpose. We do not ship assets intended for harassment RP. Moderators/maintainers may reject scenario PRs that violate this policy.
